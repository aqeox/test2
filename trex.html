L E G A C Y


Mysterious and thrustful light cycles run alongside a boasting dark chassis to feed into an active and highly-adaptable suspension system with exposed low-profile grid tires. Following an aesthetic engineering philosophy, T-REX explores the boundaries between reality and science fiction.
