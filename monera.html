STREAMLINED STEALTH CHASSIS


Monera’s multi-layered Chassis is a novelty in the supercar industry. Built around a carbon fiber tub, a specially-shaped frame contains advanced materials which translate into revolutionary stealth capabilities. Over the years of development, the aerodynamic specifications matured and new features such as ring seal technology were added to make Monera supercruise-ready.




CONSISTENT RESPONSIVENESS


A naturally-aspirated V12 machine delivers consistent power while featuring unmatched responsiveness to environmental challenges. Monera’s high-tech combustion process achieves outstanding trustless thermal efficiency while unleashing violent kinetic energy. The increased horsepower and torque makes it almost impossible to capture the biest. Her propulsion system is secured by a sophisticated XMR computing system and supported by more than a 100 sensors.
