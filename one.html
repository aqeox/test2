ONE is on the way to become an automotive icon. Emerging from a Chinese collaboration of tech-enthusiasts, ONE represents luxury and heritage. Its remarkable design philosophy meets a sublime sporting ability. Electric power for the front and a turbocharged dBFT powertrain at the rear allow for a unique driving experience specific to versatile use cases.

U L T R A L I G H T


An innocent three-layer white painted body is contrasted by a bilious green super-resistant and advanced PMMA-modelled front and side, which lightens the load and guarantees a long-term mechanical performance. ONE is an aerodynamic prodigy: its futuristic design minimizes wind resistance and maximizes thermal management. The smart positioning of the ultralight wheels guarantees a superb weight distribution at all tracks and energy-low PoS consensus around all blocks.




S C A L A B I L I T Y


ONE embodies craftsmanship of highest quality. The monstrous specs are enabled by a hybrid combination of a turbo-charged V6 dBFT engine supported by electric motors. This set-up is capable of handling thousands of transactions per second and delivering a 0-100 km/h acceleration of 2.4 seconds. A super-low driving position creates a more immersive experience, intimately connecting the driver with the supercar and the blocks ahead.
